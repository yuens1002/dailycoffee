  • WHAT'S NEW - 09.12.14
    Fall is getting close! Not to be missed, have 2 single origin coffees by Herkimer. Need some fruits? Try our furit bowel medley. Did we hear waffles? You got it. Want a little sparkle in your drink? No problem, try our seasonal fruit puree Bellini.   • SIGNATURE
    Hemy, Miles, Summit???? Just a few names we gave our signature drinks which took more than a year to have finally made it onto the menu. Many of our signiture drinks are variations of the Italian classics made with non-dairy alteratives by the help of our regulars. We think you'll love these creative takes on drinks you already know. While you're at it, try a Mocha with our house chocolate sauce made from scratch without the use of dairy or persevatives.
  • COFFEE
    Taste the coffee! We're very proud to be serving cofffee roasted locally by Herkimer. They source their green beans grown naturally in shaded areas from old world farms where no pesticides or fertilizers are used. Their uncompromising standards in coffee selection combined with their meticulous roasting methods ensure that we're always getting the best of the crop twice a week delivered to our doors.
  • TEA / CHAI
    We serve a variety of whole leaf organic and fair trade certified teas provided by Choice Tea just around the corner in Georgetown. For the Summer, we're now serving Arnold Palmer (iced tea with lemonade) and a Thai iced tea that are perfect for those hot thirsty days ahead. Need a little spice for your drink? Try our Chai teas by David Rio, two distintive blends are available - the Tiger Chai (creamy/sweet) or the Power Chai (spciy).
  • FOOD
    Our selection of breakfast and lunch sandwiches are all prepared in-house then made to order with minimally processed or fresh ingridents. You'll definitely taste the love we put in the food. Custom requests on available ingridents are welcomed, just let us know what your preferrences are at the window.
  • PASTRIES
    We offer a small selection of fine pastries from local sources such Rustie's Famous Cheesecake, Flying Apron's peanut butter bars, and our very own homemade cookies and seasonal baked goods by Diana. Be sure to like us on Facebook or follow us on Twitter. All of our updates on pastries will be updated there.

A bit of History

The lot where our building is sitting on now once stood a restaurant called "Koffee Pot". The restaurant had been taken down years ago and the lot had been left vaccant. We contacted the landlord in early Spring of 2012 and the rest is history. Daily Coffee & Tea Co. offically opened its doors (driveway) on Sep 25, 2012 with about a year in planning and hours of labor.

Meet the Owners

Sunny & Diana

Sunny & Diana are coffee & tea enthusiasts who frequent local cafes and restaurants. It has always been a dream since they met to have a coffee shop of their own one day. The two knocked around the idea for years and years before opening Daily Coffee & Tea Co. which was supposed to be a hobby that the two can do to grow old together. Brick by brick, from one person to another, they built a perfect place for stories to be told and experiences to be remembered. Sunny now manages the shop and Diana creates the seasonal and specialty home baked goods. If you're lucky, you may catch up with them from time to time on a Sunday afternoon...
